My University Projects
3rd Year Project

In the third year of my university course, I undertook a project to establish a functional network using CISCO Packet Tracer. In this endeavor, I implemented various systems, including VLANs, VPNs, and fault tolerance methods. The network boasts essential servers such as a DNS server, Mailing server, and a dedicated server for managing IPs and VLANs. Additionally, NAT/PAT has been seamlessly integrated into the network for enhanced functionality.

Here we can see an image of our finished project, the coloured boxes outlineing our differnt VLANs, with the rounded boxes reporesenting our servers, and Additional networks outside of our main one.

4th year projects

Within fourth year of my course, I developed an application, the purpose of this application is to allow me the ability to use recognition software on animal pictures uploaded to S3 buckets, providing me with a similarity percentage.
